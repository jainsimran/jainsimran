About

I think everything is possible if you believe in yourself and approach each design opportunity as a challenge to do your best work. I always encourage myself to face these challenges in my personal and professional life. As a interactive designer, I believe things we create should have some connection, either emotional or intellectual (or both) to the target audience. If the work fails to connect, we have failed to find the right solution. I enjoy creating in a variety of milieu and platforms especially front end coding and UI/UX.
